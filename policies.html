Policies & Terms

Please read these points carefully before buying any digital notes or ebooks from Edutaxgrid. We keep things clear so you always know what to expect.

Digital-only products UPI payments No physical delivery

1. Type of products

Edutaxgrid sells only digital study material, such as:

  • PDF notes and ebooks
  • Question banks, summaries and short notes
  • Other downloadable academic content

There is no physical book or printed copy included in any order.

2. Delivery & time

After we receive your UPI payment and payment confirmation form, we will send your files to the email address you provided.

  • Usual delivery time: within 36 hours.
  • Files are sent as PDF attachments or secure download links to your email.
  • Please also check your spam / promotions folder.

3. No refund policy (digital content)

Once a digital file is shared with you, it cannot be “returned”. For this reason, all purchases on Edutaxgrid are non-refundable.

We do not provide refund in cases like:

  • “I don’t need the notes anymore.”
  • “I bought this by mistake.”
  • “I thought it was some other subject.”

We may help you in cases like:

  • You paid the correct amount but did not receive any email within 36 hours.
  • The files sent are clearly corrupted or not opening at all.

In such cases, we will first try to re-send or fix the files using the same email used during order.

4. Use of content

All notes and ebooks are meant only for your personal study use. You are not allowed to:

  • Re-sell or re-upload our notes on other websites.
  • Share them publicly in groups or channels for free or paid.
  • Claim that you are the original creator of our content.

You may keep a copy for your own study, take printouts for yourself, and use them for your exams and preparation.

5. Payments & order confirmation

  • All payments are currently accepted only through UPI.
  • After paying, you must fill the payment form on our website so we can match your transaction to your email.
  • If the amount paid and the order details do not match, we may contact you on the email / phone number you provided.

6. Mistakes in email or details

If you entered a wrong email, we may not be able to deliver your files. In that case:

  • You should contact us quickly using the feedback form or reply to any email you received from Edutaxgrid.
  • We may ask for proof of payment and some basic verification before re-sending the files.

7. Changes to notes & updates

Sometimes we may update or improve a digital product (for example, fixing a typo or adding extra pages).

  • We may send updated versions to recent buyers whenever possible, but it is not guaranteed.
  • Syllabus changes with time; users are responsible for checking if the material still matches their current syllabus.

8. Contact & support

If you have any issue with your order (payment done but not received notes, wrong file, etc.), please:

  • Use the feedback form on our home page, or
  • Reply to the email from which you received your files.

We aim to respond as soon as possible, but please allow some time especially during exam seasons.
